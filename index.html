About Us

Geckery is a pioneering venture founded by Nathaniel Comiskey, Joshua Suprenant, and Ben Kudaraskas, driven by a passion for blending affordability with craftsmanship in the PC case industry. Our team is dedicated to disrupting the market with our innovative approach, leveraging cutting-edge technology to deliver high-quality wooden cases that stand out for their style and sustainability.

Our Mission

At Geckery, our mission is simple yet profound: to redefine the PC case experience by offering affordable, eco-friendly, and stylish alternatives to traditional materials. We strive to empower users to make a statement with their builds while enjoying the performance and functionality they deserve, all without compromising on quality or breaking the bank
